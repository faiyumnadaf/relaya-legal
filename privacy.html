Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Relaya ("we", "our", "us") provides a WhatsApp Team Inbox platform for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our messaging service, including business account details, WhatsApp message content routed through our platform, and contact information of customers who message connected business numbers.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to deliver, maintain, and improve our team inbox service, route messages between businesses and their customers, and provide customer support.

WhatsApp Business Platform

Relaya uses the official WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta Cloud API). Message data is processed in accordance with Meta's terms and WhatsApp Business policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with service providers necessary to operate the platform (such as Meta for message delivery) and as required by law.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect stored data.

Data Retention

We retain message and account data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
